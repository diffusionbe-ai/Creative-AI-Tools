Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

At Diff AI Studio, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website and use our services related to silent creativity and visual emergence tools.

We are committed to transparency in our data practices and compliance with GDPR and other applicable privacy regulations. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide and improve our services. This includes technical data such as IP addresses, browser types, device information, and pages visited. We may also collect information you voluntarily provide when contacting us or subscribing to updates.

Our focus on mindful creation extends to data collection—we only gather what is essential for delivering a seamless experience with our artistic intuition and aesthetic transformation tools.

Use of Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and understand user preferences. Cookies help us remember your settings and provide personalized content related to inner imagery and visual emergence.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of our website. We use both session cookies (temporary) and persistent cookies (stored on your device) for functionality and analytics purposes.

Analytics and Tracking

We utilize analytics services to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our content about silent creativity, idea formation, and abstract perception. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymized to protect your privacy.

These tools may collect information about your visit patterns, time spent on pages, and navigation paths. This data helps us optimize the user experience for those exploring artistic intuition and mindful creation resources.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating our website, conducting business, or serving users, provided they agree to keep information confidential.

Any third-party services we use are carefully selected and required to maintain appropriate security measures to protect your data in accordance with privacy regulations.

Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have specific rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access your personal information
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security practices include encryption, secure servers, and regular security assessments.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We continuously update our security measures to maintain the highest standards of data protection.
